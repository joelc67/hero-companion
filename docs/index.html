Hero Companion

a free, offline sidekick for City of Heroes: Homecoming — build planning, leveling, and play intel from the game's own data

Hero Companion — the full app

Design and optimize builds from a first-principles model of the game's combat math, level 1–50 with a per-step walkthrough, plan respecs with a working checklist, and turn your play sessions into insight. Green P in your tray.

Companion Litealpha

The little brother: one small exe, no install, whose only job is capturing your game logs into local intel that feeds your Pulse Boards. Captures every account with logging on, adds an optional in-game menu for one-click logging, and runs safely alongside the full app. Blue P in your tray. Everything stays on your machine.

CoH Pulse Boardsalpha

Community intel for the game we actually play — server pulse, scorecards, and eventually market prices, League run pages, and supergroup standings. Right now it's a single-member alpha; every future stat will be individually opt-in and anonymous by default.
